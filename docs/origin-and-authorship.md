## Original Project Scope

AIPOU combines these project-specific elements:

- an MCP-first lifecycle for beginning and completing AI-assisted tasks;
- a unique nonce and wallet authorization for a task;
- local hashes instead of publishing raw prompts and outputs;
- signed work receipts with replay and duplicate-evidence checks;
- validator-derived eligibility and trust policy;
- optional Merkle-batched claims for AIPOU rewards on Base;
- portable `receiptId` and `workReceiptId` references for integrations.

This declaration documents AIPOU's implementation, specification, name, and
publication history. It does not claim ownership of general concepts, public
standards, third-party software, cryptographic primitives, or prior work.
