# Robinhood Agentic Trading Outreach - July 22, 2026

## Why This Is Relevant

Robinhood's public Agentic Trading documentation exposes a Trading MCP for
third-party AI agents. It also states that Robinhood does not control,
supervise, monitor, recommend, or audit those third-party agents. AIPOU can be
evaluated as a complementary, issuer-asserted audit artifact around the agent
workflow without claiming to replace Robinhood's execution records or safety
controls.

Official path:

- https://robinhood.com/us/en/agentic-trading/
- https://robinhood.com/us/en/support/articles/agentic-trading-overview/
- https://go.robinhood.com/partner

## Submitted Positioning

The partnership proposal presents AIPOU as an MCP-first receipt protocol for
recording user-authorized AI work around high-consequence actions. It proposes
a synthetic-data pilot that correlates authorization, an agent-observed MCP
result, and a broker-issued reference while keeping those evidence classes
separate.

The proposal does not present AIPOU as a trading token, investment strategy,
broker, custody service, order-execution proof, or incentive to trade. Optional
human rewards remain outside the pilot and must never depend on trading volume,
order count, gains, losses, or asset choice.
