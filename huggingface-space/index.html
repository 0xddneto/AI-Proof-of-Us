HOW IT WORKS

Proof before reward

The protocol records authorization and completion without publishing raw prompts or outputs.

  1. 01

    Authorize

    A dedicated wallet signs a unique task nonce using EIP-712.

  2. 02

    Observe

    The client records usage hashes and a local Ed25519 collector signs the receipt.

  3. 03

    Validate

    Signatures, nonces, duplicate evidence, and the derived trust tier are checked.

  4. 04

    Claim

    Approved receipts enter a Merkle batch and AIPOUClaims mints the reward.
